SOP/LOR

SOP:
An SOP stands for Statement of Purpose.The evaluator has never seen you or heard of you. So, the SoP serves the purpose of telling the admission committee why they should choose you.It is “the” most important document.
A well written SoP, particularly for the top universities in US for MS courses, can tilt the scales in your favour.

LOR:
Letter of Recommendation (LoR) helps the admission committee know about your technical, academic and non-academic expertise through your recommender. There are certain areas on which the universities expect you to be rated by your receommender.
